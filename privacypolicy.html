Privacy Policy

Last updated: 02/09/2024

At Gandhinagar Sports Academy, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services. Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our services, including:

1.1 Personal Identification Information

When you register on our platform, book services, or interact with us in any way, we may collect personal identification information that can be used to identify you personally. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Name: Your full name, as required for registration or booking purposes.
  • Email Address: Your email address, which we use to communicate with you.
  • Phone Number: Your contact number, used for confirmation and communication.
  • Payment Information: Details necessary for processing payments, such as credit/debit card numbers or bank account information. This information is processed by secure payment gateways and is not stored on our servers.
  • Date of Birth: To verify your age and eligibility for certain programs.
  • Emergency Contact Information: Details of a person to contact in case of an emergency during your participation in our activities.

1.2 Non-Personal Identification Information

We may also collect non-personal identification information about users whenever they interact with our website. Non-personal identification information may include:

  • Browser Information: The browser name, type of computer, and technical information about your means of connection to our site, such as the operating system and the Internet service providers used.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our services, such as the pages you view, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other diagnostic data.
  • Location Data: We may collect information about your location if you enable this feature on your device. This helps us to provide location-specific services.

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information from you in several ways:

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you register for an account, book a service, subscribe to our newsletter, participate in a survey, contact us for customer support, or otherwise interact with our services.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

We may automatically collect information about you when you use our services, such as usage data, IP address, and device information. We may also use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect information about your interaction with our website.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third parties, such as social media platforms, when you choose to log in or register through them. This information may include your social media profile information, such as your name, email address, and profile picture.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

3.1 To Provide and Manage Our Services

We use your personal information to deliver the services you have requested, manage your bookings, process payments, and provide customer support. This includes using your information to:

  • Process Transactions: We use your payment information to complete your purchase and provide you with the services you have requested.
  • Create and Manage Accounts: We use your information to create and manage your user account, ensuring you have access to the appropriate features and services.
  • Communicate with You: We use your contact information to send you service-related communications, such as booking confirmations, updates, and customer support responses.

3.2 To Improve Our Services

We use the information we collect to understand how our services are used, identify areas for improvement, and enhance the overall user experience. This includes:

  • Analyzing Usage Patterns: We analyze usage data to understand how our users interact with our services and identify trends and patterns.
  • Developing New Features: We use feedback and usage data to develop new features and improve existing ones.

3.3 To Personalize Your Experience

We may use your information to personalize the content and offers you see on our platform, tailoring our services to better suit your preferences and interests. This includes:

  • Customized Content: We may use your browsing history and preferences to show you content that is more relevant to your interests.
  • Targeted Offers: We may use your information to provide you with special offers and promotions that are tailored to your interests.

3.4 To Comply with Legal Obligations

We may use your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes. This includes:

  • Regulatory Compliance: We may use your information to comply with financial reporting obligations and other regulatory requirements.
  • Legal Requests: We may disclose your information in response to lawful requests by public authorities, including law enforcement agencies.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. However, we may share your information with third parties in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted service providers who perform functions on our behalf, such as payment processing, data analysis, email delivery, hosting services, customer service, and marketing assistance. These service providers have access to your personal information only to perform these tasks and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

4.2 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or other sale of all or a portion of our assets, your personal information may be transferred to the acquiring entity. We will notify you of any such change in ownership or transfer of assets to a new entity.

4.3 Legal Compliance

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities, such as a court or a government agency. This includes:

  • Complying with Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information if we believe it is necessary to comply with a legal obligation or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others.
  • Enforcing Our Policies: We may disclose your information as necessary to enforce our terms and policies, or to investigate and prevent fraud or other illegal activities.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your data. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5.1 Security Measures

We use a combination of technical, administrative, and physical controls to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure. These measures include:

  • Encryption: We use encryption protocols to secure data transmitted over the Internet.
  • Access Controls: We restrict access to personal information to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security audits to identify and address potential vulnerabilities in our systems.

5.2 Data Retention

We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. When we no longer need to use your information and there is no need to keep it for legal or regulatory reasons, we will either remove it from our systems or anonymize it so that it cannot be linked back to you.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information, and we offer you choices about how we collect, use, and share your information. These rights include:

6.1 Accessing and Correcting Your Information

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you and to request that we correct any inaccuracies. You can access your account information at any time by logging into your account on our website.

6.2 Data Portability

You have the right to request a copy of your personal information in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, and to request that we transfer this information to another service provider, where technically feasible.

6.3 Deleting Your Information

You have the right to request that we delete your personal information. We will comply with your request unless we are required to retain the information for legal or regulatory reasons.

6.4 Opting Out of Marketing Communications

You can opt out of receiving marketing communications from us at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in the communication or by contacting us directly.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18 without parental consent. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete the information as soon as possible. If you believe that we may have collected information from a child under 18, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes to this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of the policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
