Refund Policy

Last updated: 02/09/2024

At Gandhinagar Sports Academy, we are committed to providing high-quality services to our clients. We understand that sometimes plans change, and you may need to cancel a booking. This refund policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds are granted for services purchased through our platform.

1. General Refund Policy

Our general refund policy applies to all services booked through Gandhinagar Sports Academy. Refunds are subject to the following conditions:

  • Cancellation Period: Refunds are only available for cancellations made at least 7 days before the scheduled service date. Cancellations made within 6 days of the service date are non-refundable.
  • Payment Method: Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used at the time of booking. Please allow 7-10 business days for the refund to be processed.
  • Non-Refundable Services: Certain services may be non-refundable. These services will be clearly marked as non-refundable at the time of booking.

2. Special Circumstances

We understand that unexpected events can occur. The following special circumstances may warrant a refund, even if they fall outside of the general refund policy:

  • Medical Emergencies: If you or an immediate family member experience a medical emergency that prevents you from attending the service, we may offer a refund upon receipt of appropriate documentation.
  • Weather-Related Cancellations: In the event of severe weather conditions that result in the cancellation of a service by Gandhinagar Sports Academy, a full refund will be offered, or the service may be rescheduled at your convenience.
  • Instructor or Venue Issues: If a service is canceled due to issues with the instructor or venue, a full refund will be provided, or the service may be rescheduled.

3. How to Request a Refund

If you are eligible for a refund, you can request it by following these steps:

  • Contact Us: Email us at info@gandhinagarsportsacademy.com with your booking details and the reason for the refund request.
  • Provide Documentation: If applicable, provide any necessary documentation to support your refund request (e.g., a medical certificate for medical emergencies).
  • Processing Time: Once we receive your request, we will review it and respond within 5 business days. If approved, your refund will be processed within 7-10 business days.

4. Non-Refundable Scenarios

Refunds will not be granted in the following scenarios:

  • No-Show: If you do not show up for the service without prior cancellation, no refund will be provided.
  • Late Cancellation: Cancellations made within 7 days of the service date are non-refundable unless covered by special circumstances as outlined above.
  • Unmet Expectations: Refunds will not be provided for services that meet the description provided at the time of booking but do not meet subjective personal expectations.

5. Changes to This Refund Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this refund policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. Your continued use of our services after any changes to this policy have been posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised terms.
